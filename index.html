About

Data, maps,
and politics.

I'm Mattia, a researcher and analyst based in Florence. I work with electoral and spatial data, combining GIS tools with statistical analysis to understand how political behaviour varies across urban and suburban territories.

My current focus is on Florence and Sesto Fiorentino, where I am building a granular database of voting results at the sezione elettorale level across multiple elections — from the 2024 Comunali to the 2025 Regionali.

I work primarily in R for data processing and spatial joins, QGIS and ArcGIS Pro for cartography, and open-web tools for interactive publishing.
